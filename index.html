About Us

Premium berries from the clean forests of Finland.

Wild Finnish Berries is a Finnish berry brand specializing in natural berries from Finland. We focus on quality, sustainability and carefully selected products for customers in Finland and internationally.

Our berries are handled with care to preserve their natural taste, freshness and nutritional value. We serve wholesale buyers, restaurants, food distributors and export partners.
